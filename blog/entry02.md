## Apps 
Some Apps I've found that used by Graphic Designer to make their design are

* PicMonkey: Its a designing app and website simliar to canva. Its helos users create designs easily by providing video tutorials on editing ans a page dedicated to graphic design. The app is user friendly and offers many features, from photo editing to advanced graphic designing.
* GIMP: It is a free, advanced photo editing software that includes tools such as layers,masks,filters,and plug ins,its similar to Adobe photoshop. It works on both Windows and macOS and requires only basic hardware. GIMP is popular with photographers and digital artists fot its features.
* Visme: Its an online design and presentation tools that helps users create reports,social media graphics,animations, and more. It is fully cloud-based and offers templates,drag and drop design tools, and collaboration features forteams. Visme works on any device with a web browser and making it more easy and convenient for designers anywhere.
